# Arkansas Innovation Hub Cooperative
## Foundation & Corporate Partnerships

### Fundraising Philosophy

The Hub's fundraising strategy positions foundation and corporate support as **startup capital and capacity building investment**, not operational subsidy. The earned income model demonstrates long-term sustainability, making the Hub an attractive investment for mission-aligned funders seeking community impact with financial viability.

### Year 1 Fundraising Goal: $153,768

- Technology infrastructure: $10,000

**Critical Message**: These funds enable launch and sustainability, not ongoing operations. Earned income covers all recurring costs.

#### Proposal Development Process

**Letter of Inquiry (LOI)**:
- 2-3 page introduction to project
- Problem statement and solution
- Organizational capacity and sustainability
- Specific funding request and use
- Preliminary budget and timeline

**Full Proposal Components**:
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Organizational Background (1-2 pages)
3. Problem Statement (1-2 pages)
4. Project Description and Methods (2-3 pages)
5. Evaluation and Outcomes (1-2 pages)
6. Sustainability Plan (1 page)
7. Budget and Budget Narrative (2-3 pages)
8. Appendices (board list, financials, letters of support)

**Standard Attachments**:
- IRS determination letter (once obtained)
- Board of Directors list with affiliations
- Current year budget and financial statements
- Letters of support from community partners
- Project photos and facility images
- Organizational chart and staff qualifications

### Case for Support Messaging

#### Core Narratives

**Preservation of Federal Investment**:
"The Arkansas Regional Innovation Hub represents a $3.5 million federal Economic Development Administration investment in North Little Rock's economic future. The cooperative model ensures this critical community asset serves its intended purpose for generations to come."

**Community Ownership Creates Sustainability**:
"Unlike traditional nonprofit models dependent on unpredictable grants, the cooperative structure creates member ownership and earned income sustainability. Your investment enables launch, but the community ensures long-term success."

**Economic Development Impact**:
"Makerspaces generate measurable economic impact: entrepreneur business launches, prototype-to-production pathways, workforce skill development, and downtown revitalization. The Hub serves as infrastructure for Arkansas' innovation economy."

**Educational Excellence**:
"Arkansas students deserve access to hands-on STEAM education that prepares them for advanced manufacturing, engineering, and technology careers. The Hub provides resources few schools can afford, democratizing access to tools and training."

**Equity and Access**:
"The cooperative model ensures affordable access regardless of income. Student memberships at $25/month, scholarship programs, and school partnerships remove barriers that exclude low-income families from maker education."

### Donor Cultivation Strategy

#### Relationship Building

**Foundation Officers**:
- Request informational meetings before proposal submission
- Invite to facility tours and open houses
- Provide regular email updates on progress
- Share success stories and impact metrics
- Acknowledge publicly when appropriate

**Corporate Decision-Makers**:
- Identify champions within target companies
- Offer exclusive behind-the-scenes experiences
- Engage in industry-relevant discussions
- Invite to advisory roles or committees
- Demonstrate ROI through workforce and community impact

**Individual Major Donors** ($5,000+):
- Personal meetings with Executive Director and board members
- Special recognition opportunities (naming rights)
- Involvement in program development
- Invitation to exclusive events
- Regular impact reporting and gratitude

#### Stewardship & Recognition

**Foundation Funders**:
- Timely grant reporting as required
- Mid-grant updates on progress
- Invitation to visit and see impact
- Featured in annual report
- Public acknowledgment as appropriate

**Corporate Partners**:

- Annual appreciation event

**Individual Donors**:
