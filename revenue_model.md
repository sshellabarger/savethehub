# Arkansas Innovation Hub Cooperative
## Revenue Model & Financial Projections

### Revenue Philosophy

The Hub's financial model prioritizes **earned income sustainability** over grant dependency. Year 1 earned income of $419,006 covers 100% of core operating expenses, with grants funding separate restricted programs and foundation support providing growth capital.

### Revenue Growth Assumptions

**Conservative Projections**:
- Membership growth: 5% annually after Year 1 base
- Class attendance: 80% capacity utilization
- Fabrication services: Modest $1,500-2,000/month
- No assumptions about viral growth or major corporate accounts

**Risk Mitigation**:
- Multiple small revenue streams vs. single large source
- Earned income covers core operations, grants are supplemental
- Member retention focus vs. constant new acquisition
- Gradual growth model vs. aggressive expansion

### Pricing Strategy

**Membership Tiers**:
- Priced below commercial makerspace rates ($80 vs $100-200+)
- Student discount encourages youth participation
- Family rate incentivizes household engagement
- Corporate rate provides business partnership value

**Education Programs**:
- Class pricing competitive with community college non-credit courses
- Volume discounts for school district partnerships
- Scholarship availability for income-qualified students
- Revenue sharing aligns instructor incentives

**Fabrication Services**:
- Market rate pricing for commercial work
- Member discounts encourage utilization
- Materials markup covers inventory costs
- Hourly rates reflect skilled operator value

### Earned Income Metrics

**Key Performance Indicators**:

*Membership*:
- Member retention rate: >85% annually
- New member acquisition: 25-30/month (Months 1-6)
- Member satisfaction: >90% positive ratings
- Facility utilization: >70% during operating hours

*Education*:
- Class fill rate: >80% capacity
- Repeat student rate: >60%
- Instructor revenue share: 30% of gross tuition
- Student satisfaction: >4.5/5 average rating

*Fabrication*:
- Monthly service revenue: $1,500-2,500
- Project margin: >50% after labor and materials
- Customer acquisition cost: <$100/new commercial account
- Repeat customer rate: >70%

**Financial Health Metrics**:
- Earned income covers 100% core operations
- Operating reserve: Minimum 6 months expenses
- Debt service coverage ratio: >1.5x
- Revenue diversification: No single source >40% of total
