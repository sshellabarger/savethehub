## Community Campaign to Preserve the Arkansas Regional Innovation Hub

**Status:** Active Campaign  
**Target:** Community acquisition of 201 E. Broadway, North Little Rock, AR  
**Timeline:** January-June 2026  
**Structure:** Member-owned Cooperative (501c3)

---

## ğŸ“‹ Campaign Overview

The Arkansas Regional Innovation Hub was built in 2015 with $1 million in federal taxpayer funds (EDA Grant #08-01-04368) to serve as Arkansas's premier makerspace and STEAM education facility. After serving 71,000+ people across 20 Arkansas counties from 2014-2025, the Hub faces closure as Winrock International plans to sell the property.

**Our Mission:** Acquire the Hub for community ownership through a member-owned cooperative structure, ensuring continued service through the federal grant's 2035 requirement while building a financially sustainable, democratically-controlled community asset.

---

## ğŸ¯ Campaign Goals

1. **Acquire Property:** Purchase 201 E. Broadway for community ownership
2. **Establish Cooperative:** Create member-owned 501(c)(3) cooperative
3. **Ensure Sustainability:** Build earned-income focused financial model
4. **Maintain Federal Compliance:** Honor EDA grant requirements through October 2035
5. **Serve Community:** Continue STEAM education, maker services, economic development
